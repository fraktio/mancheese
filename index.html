What is Mancheese?

Mancheese is an exclusive delicacy that is highly appraised for its sophisticated taste which is originated from its unique raw material: the male breast milk. Male breast milk has unique properties that makes it not only very demanding but also rewarding raw material for coagulation.

There is of course many steps when making cheese and most of these doesn't really differ much from making more mundane types of cheese. One that needs to be mentioned is that our Mancheese is often beer washed. All of our cheeses are cave aged at least one full year before they are considered ready for consumption. Taste of Mancheese is usually described as 'strong', 'full' and 'rich'.

In short the Mancheese is a cheese of exceptional quality made from male breast milk.

Male breast milk?
